2018 PI4 Computational Bootcamp

The 2018 PI4 computational Bootcamp will be held May 21 – May 26, 2017 in 239 Altgeld Hall.

The PI4 bootcamp is a six-day intensive course for for PI4 students prior to beginning summer internships as part of the NSF funded PI4 Program for Interdisciplinary and Industrial Internships at Illinois.
